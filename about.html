About us

itBoat is a global database of all existing models of sailing and motor yachts and a web service which helps search, choose, compare and buy boats. itBoat partners with experienced and trusted yacht sellers and connects buyers with sellers through Fantastic Yacht Catalog to ensure the most seamless buying experience.
